## 8. AI Tools for Marketing

### Meta AI (Facebook/Instagram)
- Built into Meta Ads Manager
- Auto-generates ad copy and audience suggestions
- Useful when running Instagram/Facebook ads
- **Free with Meta Ads account — use when you start paid ads**

### Apollo AI
- B2B lead generation tool
- **NOT needed for your use case** (you're targeting students, not businesses)
- Skip this
